## 📊 Project Overview
This project presents an interactive Tableau dashboard designed to analyze marketing campaign performance and customer behavior. It transforms raw customer data into insights related to revenue, conversions, and campaign effectiveness.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Evaluate performance of different marketing campaigns  
- Analyze customer behavior and spending patterns  
- Identify high-performing products and channels  
- Understand relationship between income and spending  
- Support data-driven marketing decisions  

---

## ⚙️ Dashboard Features
- KPI cards for total revenue and conversion rate  
- Campaign-wise performance comparison (Bar Charts)  
- Product popularity analysis  
- Scatter plot for income vs spending correlation  
- Global filters for demographic analysis  
- Clean and user-friendly dashboard design  

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies Used
- Tableau (Data Visualization)  
- Excel (Data Preparation)  

---

## 📈 Key Insights
- Identified top-performing marketing campaigns based on revenue and conversions  
- Highlighted products with highest customer demand  
- Observed correlation between customer income and spending behavior  
- Segmented high-value customers based on demographic filters  
- Identified most effective sales channels  

---

## 🚀 Project Outcome
Developed an interactive dashboard that converts marketing data into **actionable insights**, helping businesses optimize campaigns, target high-value customers, and improve conversion strategies.
